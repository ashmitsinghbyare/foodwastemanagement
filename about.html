About Us

We are a team of passionate individuals committed to addressing the issue of food waste in India. Our goal is to create a system that connects food donors with charities and NGOs, while also reducing the environmental impact of food waste.

About Sagar

Sagar is a historic city in Madhya Pradesh, known for its educational heritage and beautiful landscapes. Located centrally, it serves as an important location for our initiative. We collaborate with local organizations and donors to reduce food waste and feed the needy.
